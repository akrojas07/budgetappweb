Welcome to Mi Feria!

Why "Mi Feria"

In spanish, the term "Feria" roughly translates to cash and "Mi Feria" translates to my money. The Mi Feria Budgeting Application is all about learning to own your money and take control of your financial situation!

Money is not scary

At some point, someone said money was scary and everyone agreed. But money is not scary, and taking control of your financial situation shouldn't be either. Once you accept this, meeting your short and long term financial goals will be as easy as pie.

"Budget" is not a bad word

Through the Mi Feria process, you will learn that budget is not a bad word! In fact, your budget will become your best friend. As you progress, you will learn to use your budget to achieve your short and long term financial goals.
